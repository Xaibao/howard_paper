#6  FedAvg 公式——聚合的是權重還是梯度？
----------------------------------------------
【確認】FedAvg 聚合的是模型權重（model weights/parameters），不是梯度。

【建議回覆】
FedAvg aggregates model weights (parameters), not gradients. The aggregation
formula is:

    w_global = Σ (n_i / n_total) × w_i

where w_i denotes the local model weights of the i-th Edge Client and n_i is
the number of local training samples. After each FL round, the aggregated global
weights are broadcast back to all clients. Raw data never leave the local site,
ensuring data privacy.


#7  high_band_loss 是否在損失函數中？
----------------------------------------------
【確認】程式碼確認存在：
    total_loss = slope_loss + 1.0×linear_loss + 10.0×recon_loss + 12.0×high_band_loss

【建議回覆】
Yes, high_band_loss is included in the CGAN loss function with the highest
weight (12.0). It penalises reconstruction error in the near-infrared high-band
region (850–1000 nm), forcing the generator to faithfully reproduce the
absorption characteristics that distinguish FOG contaminants in that spectral
range. The full loss is:

    L_total = L_slope + 1.0 × L_linear + 10.0 × L_recon + 12.0 × L_high_band

----------------------------------------------
【FL 實際執行數據（2026-06-16）】

  Client : 1 node（client_01），本地資料 261 筆（208 訓練 / 53 驗證）

【建議回覆（放入論文 ch5 / Reviewer Response）】
The proposed federated learning framework was evaluated over 10 communication
rounds using one edge client (client_01) with 261 local samples. Starting from
the pre-trained MLP-Transformer weights, the global model achieved 100.00%
accuracy in Round 1 and maintained above 98% throughout training, with only a
minor fluctuation to 98.11% in Round 3. Training loss steadily decreased from
0.413 (Round 1) to a minimum of 0.393 (Round 9), demonstrating stable
convergence within 4 rounds. These results confirm that the FedAvg-based
aggregation effectively preserves classification performance while keeping raw
spectral data on-site at each monitoring station.
